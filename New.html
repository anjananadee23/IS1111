Part A

Located primarily in the Colombo metropolitan
region, the University of Colombo is structured
as a state institution which provides access to
higher education across the country. The main campus
is sited in the heart of Colombo, on a sprawling
50-acre estate, and comprises contemporary urban
constructions, as well as old colonial architecture,
considered national heritage sites.Top
